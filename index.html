Tracks

Connect the World (IOT Challenge)

IoT Challenge is a one-of- a-kind platform where participants give a real form to their creative IoT solutions. The uniqueness and beauty of this challenge is that there will not be a particular problem statement. The teams can ideate an IoT solution in any of the facets of human life to make it simpler, easier, more convenient, or more systematic. The teams can explore their minds in a wide range of fields.

Feed the Nation

The challenge is to develop innovative processes, methods, tools, devices, techniques, procedures and service delivery models to improve nutrition outcomes. Improvement in outcomes should be measurable, achievable and sustainable.

Open Innovation

Have an out of the box idea which does not fall into the other streams, just innovate here. You could build anything. This track is for all those crazy ideas built using niche technologies today for a better tomorrow.
